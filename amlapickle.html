A m l a P i c k l e

Ingredients:

Gooseberry:1kg

Red Chilli Powder:100grms

Salt:100grms

Turmeric powder:10grms

Mustard Seeds:20grms

Fenugreek Seeds:15grms

Garlic:50grms

Groundnut Oil:1ltr

    Preparation:

    Firstly,clean the mangoes

    and dry them and make sure that there is no wet.

    And boil them until they turn soft.

    Remove the cover of the garlics

    and smash them as a paste

    After that remove the dirt or any stones if present in

    Mustard seeds and Fenugreek seed.

    After that heat the seeds in a low flame seperately,

    and cool them and mix them as a powder.

    And take the gooseberries which are boiled and take them into a bowl

    And add turmeric powder,redchill powder,salt and garlic paste,

    and add fenugreek and mustard podwer to the bowl

    And mix them well and pour someoil into it until they dip into the oil.
