C h i c k e n c u r r y

Ingredients:

1 pound skinless bone-in chicken

½ teaspoon turmeric powder

2 teaspoons Guntur red chili powder

salt as required

1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lime juice

4 tablespoons vegetable oil

15-20 curry leaves2-3 teaspoons chopped green chilies

1 cup chopped onions

2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste

½ teaspoon garam masala powder

½ cup canned tomato puree

2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

masala powder

water as required

    Preparation:

    Heat oil in a pan. Add onion paste and ginger-garlic paste.

    Sauté this paste until golden brown.

    Add the tomato puree to the mixture in the pan.

    Then add the powder spices; coriander powder, cumin powder,

    garam masala, red chilli powder, and turmeric powder.

    Add salt as required and sauté the mixture well until the oil starts to separate.

    Add the chicken pieces to the pan and stir them
    well until they are coated in the mixture.

    Cook them until they start changing colour and turn golden brown.

    Add 1 and ½ cup of water and stir the chicken.

    Cover the pan with a lid and let the chicken cook until it turns tender.

    Once cooked, you can garnish the curry with chopped coriander.

    serve it hot with steamed basmati rice or roti.

    Serve hot..
