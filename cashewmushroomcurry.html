C a s h e w M u s h r o o m C u r r y

Ingredients:

1/4 cup Cashew nuts

3 Tomatoes Chopped

4 Garlic cloves

4 Cloves

1/4 cup Water

1 inch Ginger

3 Green chilies

2 Cardamoms

2 Red chilies

1 inch Cinnamon stick

2 cups White Button mushrooms

3/4 Cup Cashew nuts

1 cup Onion Finely chopped

1/4 tsp Turmeric powder

1/2 tsp Red chili powder/ Kashmiri red chili powder

1/2 tsp Garam masala powder

1/2 tsp Salt as per taste

1/2 tsp Coriander powder

4 tbsp Oil

Coriander leaves / cilantro hand fully

1/2 tbsp Lemon juice

    Preparation:

    Put all the ingredients for the gravy in a mixi jar and make a
    smooth, non-stick paste

    Heat 2 tbsp oil and add cashew nuts and fry until golden. Keep aside.

    Add mushrooms and cook till light golden in color and set aside.

    Add another 2 tbsp of oil to another pan ,

    add onion and cook till light golden in color

    Add the grounded paste and boil for 3-4 minutes,

    add 1/2 cup water and let the gravy thicken and the oil rise.

    Add red chili, garam masala, turmeric and coriander powder
    to gravy and mix well.

    Add enough salt to taste.

    Add fried cashews and mushrooms and cook for 3-4 minutes.

    Finally add lemon juice and coriander leaves , mix well.

    Serve with roti, chapthi or rice
