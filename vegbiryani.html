V e g B i r y a n i

Ingredients:

300g basmati rice

25g butter

1 large onion, finely sliced

Tomatoes

peas

potato

1 bay leaf

3 cardamom pods

small cinnamon stick

1 tsp turmeric

4 tbsp balti curry paste

85g raisins

850ml chicken stock

30g coriander

1/2 tsp red chili powder

1/2 tsp nutmeg powder

2 g oriental saffron

1 cup yogurt

2 tbsp ginger garlic paste

50g crushed onion & 500g fried onion

500g fried potatoes

5 green chilies cut lengthwise

    Preparation:

    Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.

    marinate in saffron, chili powder, turmeric,

    ginger-garlic paste and crushed onions.

    Add dry spices to yogurt and mix well. Marinate for 1 hour.

    Add fried onions, fried potatoes, coriander, mint leaves,

    green chilie,tomatoes,peas to the mix. Smear again.

    Gradually add oil, ghee and 2 cups of water to the mix.

    Keep aside for 30 minutes.

    Boil 1500 ml (1.5 L) of water with whole cumin, cardamom, cinnamon,

    cloves, salt and soaked rice. Boil until half done.

    Strain and place a layer of rice on mix.

    Sprinkle with fried onions, a little ghee and

    saffron diluted in 1/2 cup of hot water.

    Top with remaining rice. Sprinkle again with fried onions,

    1 tsp ghee and 1 tsp saffron.

    Seal the pot lid with the dough.

    Cook on high heat for 10 minutes, then reduce heat

    Simmer for 1 hour.

    Serve with spiced yogurt, thinly slice carrots and cucumber salad.
