L e m o n P i c k l e

Ingredients:

medium-sized lemons:10-12

salt:1/4 cup

turmeric powder:2tbsp

red chili powder:2tbsp

fenugreek seeds:1tbsp

mustard seeds:1tbsp

oil:1/4 cup

asafoetida:1tbsp

cumin seeds:1tbsp

dried red chilies:4to5

    Preparation:

    Wash and dry the lemons thoroughly.

    Cut each lemon into small, bite-sized pieces.Remove any seeds.

    Add salt and place it in a jar

    Cover the container,keep the jar about 4-5 days.

    In a dry pan, roast the fenugreek seeds until they turn slightly brown.

    Then, grind them into a fine powder using a mixer

    Heat the oil in a separate pan. Add mustard seeds.

    Then add cumin seeds, dried red chilies./p>

    Turn off the heat and add turmeric powder,

    red chili powder, and asafoetida to the hot oil.

    Once the oil mixture has cooled,

    add the ground fenugreek powder to it and mix well.

    Now, add this seasoned oil to the container of salted lemons and stir.

    are coated with the oil and spice mixture.

    Cover the container again and let it sit for a few more days

    to allow the flavors to meld together.
