C h i c k e n P i c k l e

Ingredients:

Boneless Chicken:1kg

Red Chilli Powder:250grms

Salt:200grms

Turmeric Powder:2tbsp

Ginger:100grms

Groundnut Oil:1ltr

Garam Masala:50grms

Lemon juice:2tbsp

    Preparation:

    Firstly,clean the chicken very well

    and add chicken to the pan

    and fry them until the water goes out in chicken

    after that,add oil to the other pan and heat the oil

    and add that chicken to the heated oil

    and fry them until chicken turns brown

    After clean the garlic well and mix it into a fine paste.

    After that add ginger paste to the same oil where we fried the chicken

    After that add fried chicken to the pan

    After that add redchilli powder,salt,garam masala to it

    Off the stove and cool the mix

    Add lemon juice to the pickle
