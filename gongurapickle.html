G o n g u r a P i c k l e

Ingredients:

Gongura:20leaves

Oil:4tbsp

Jeera:2tbsp

Red Chilli:10

Garlic:1

Salt:1tbsp

    Preparation:

    Firstly,take the pan and add oil into it

    and add gongura into the pan and fry them until they turn soft.

    And fry the redchilies and jeera

    and cool them and add them to a mixer.

    and also add salt,turmeric powder,garlic to the mixer

    And add gongura leaves to the mixer

    and pulse to get a coarse texture.
